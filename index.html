My Projects

Nasty Cookie Redesign

Designed a responsive user interface for both desktop and mobile, inspired by the aesthetic and branding of Nasty Cookie. While referencing their style for consistency, I also incorporated original ideas and custom features to create a unique user experience.

Study Mate – Study Group UI/UX (Group Project)

Collaborated with a team to design Study Mate, a study group interface that helps users stay motivated and connected while learning. The platform allows users to form study groups with peers and tutors, track learning progress, and access shared resources — all in one intuitive dashboard.

Food Delivery App UI/UX (Group Project)

Worked collaboratively to design a food delivery app interface inspired by platforms like GrabFood and FoodPanda. The design focused on creating a smooth and user-friendly experience for browsing food options, placing orders, and choosing payment methods.
